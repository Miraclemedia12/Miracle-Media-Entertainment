Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 10, 2026

Notice to Visitors & Advertising Partners: This document outlines our data protocols. It explicitly details how data interacts with analytics tools and conversion tracking frameworks (including Google Ads and TikTok Pixel scripts) to remain fully transparent and compliant with digital network consumer guidelines.

1. Information We Collect

At Miracle Media Entertainment, we collect information to deliver premium digital services, optimize client systems, and ensure seamless communication. This includes:

  • Personal Identity Data: Names, business email addresses, and contact phone numbers provided voluntarily via our onboarding forms or email subscription inputs.
  • Technical Device Insights: IP addresses, browser variants, operational setups, and navigation histories recorded automatically during your visit.

2. Tracking Pixels & Cookies

We implement industry-standard behavioral tracking systems to analyze our platform's reach and run efficient marketing workflows. This site employs:

  • Tracking Pixels (TikTok & Google Ads): Code fragments integrated into our frontend architecture to monitor performance metrics, document custom form conversions, and serve tailored ad placements based on past engagements.
  • Analytical Cookies: Microscopic text files deployed on your terminal to evaluate web speed trends, track layout interactivity values, and sustain user preference settings (such as dark-mode variables).

You can easily instruct your native browser to block or reject cookies completely, though doing so might affect certain responsive interface adjustments on our site.

3. How We Process Your Data

The processing of user details serves specific operational use cases:

  • To structure, test, and host custom software, websites, and database panels for clients.
  • To dispatch technical updates, system alerts, or relevant digital insights you subscribed to receive.
  • To fine-tune ad campaigns, evaluate demographic traffic pools, and secure our network infrastructure from automated form abuses.

4. Data Protection & Security Architecture

We secure user information behind strict structural safeguards. Data transmitted through our digital channels is protected using modern encryption protocols (SSL/TLS), and access to backend administrative panels or database environments is tightly restricted to authorized operators.

While we use robust encryption standards, no transmission channel over the open web can guarantee complete absolute security. We constantly audit our configurations to maintain optimal trust scores.

5. Third-Party Disclosures

We do not trade, rent, or lease private consumer metrics to third-party brokers. Data is shared strictly with reliable cloud providers, database platforms (such as Google Firebase architectures), and advertising distribution networks solely to fulfill operational tasks or match pixel tracking protocols.

6. Your Access & Control Rights

You possess complete oversight regarding your information footprints. You can request to review, adjust, or totally wipe out your records from our mailing databases at any time. To execute these steps, drop an official message to our digital desk via the contact channel below.
